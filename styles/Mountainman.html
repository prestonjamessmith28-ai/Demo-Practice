Mountain Man Banana Bread

Hearty. Rustic. Built for the wild.

Ingredients

You’ll need:

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • Mash the bananas in a sturdy wooden bowl like a true mountain man.
  • Stir in melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  • Add baking soda and salt, mixing well.
  • Add flour and fold gently until just combined.
  • Pour into a greased loaf pan.
  • Bake for 55–60 minutes until golden and fragrant.
  • Let cool, slice thick, and eat it on a mountaintop.
